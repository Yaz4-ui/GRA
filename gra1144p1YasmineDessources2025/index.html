Carvel’s Oreo Ice Cream Cake

Carvel’s Oreo Ice Cream Cake combines creamy vanilla and chocolate ice cream with Oreo cookie crumbles and Carvel’s chocolate crunchies. Topped with whipped icing and a chocolate drizzle, it’s the perfect treat for Oreo and ice cream lovers!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Cookie Pieces
  • Oreo Cookie Pieces
  • Chocolate Drizzle
  • Whipped Icing
  • Stabilizers (to maintain cake structure)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pan: Line a springform or regular pan with plastic wrap or parchment paper.
  2. First Ice Cream Layer: Spread softened vanilla ice cream evenly in the pan. Freeze for 30–60 minutes.
  3. Crunchy Layer: Mix crushed Oreo cookies with a little fudge syrup and spread over the frozen ice cream layer
  4. Second Ice Cream Layer: Spread softened chocolate ice cream on top of the cookie layer.
    5. Freeze for 2 hours.
  5. Decorate: Cover the cake with whipped icing or whipped cream. Drizzle with chocolate syrup and sprinkle with crushed Oreos.
  6. Freeze and Serve: Freeze until firm (at least 2 hours). Slice and enjoy!
